MIRO Care Hospital

MIRO Care Hospital is a health care institution providing patient treatment with specialized medical and nursing staff and medical equipment. Doctors make use of advanced medical technology to heal patients. It is typically is the major health care facility in its region, with many beds for intensive care and additional beds for patients who need long-term care. MIRO Hospital provide extensive resources and expertise that enable doctors to rapidly diagnose and treat a wide range of diseases.

Currently, MIRO Care Hospital are largely staffed by professional physicians, surgeons, nurses, and allied health practitioners, whereas in the past, this work was usually performed by the members of founding religious orders or by volunteers. However, there are various Catholic religious orders, such as the Alexians and the Bon Secours Sisters that still focus on hospital ministry in the late 1990s, as well as several other Christian denominations, including the Methodists and Lutherans, which run hospitals. In accordance with the original meaning of the word, hospitals were originally "places of hospitality", and this meaning is still preserved in the names of some institutions such as the Royal Hospital Chelsea, established in 1681 as a retirement and nursing home for veteran soldiers.
